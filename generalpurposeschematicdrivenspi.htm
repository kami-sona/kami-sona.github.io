General Purpose Schematic Driven SPICE

This is the main use of the LTspice simulator. Within the restrictions found in the license, you are free to use LTspice as your general-purpose schematic capture/SPICE program even for circuits which do not use ADI products. Many organizations standardize on LTspice as their primary design tool.

LTspice does not restrict the size or content of circuits for drafting and simulation. Marching waveforms, cross probing, reverse cross probing, and unlimited hierarchy are supported.
